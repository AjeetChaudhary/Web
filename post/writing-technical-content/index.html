Netflix is cutting down on your favourite movies

Jan 12, 2020 2 min read
Image credit: Netflix

“I need to stop” I whispered to myself, As I clicked on next episode.

Above quote explains 2 AM conversation of every Netflix user. As Netflix is focusing more on original content now, number of TV shows is increasing and number of movies is decreasing with years passing. Netflix has approximately 1100 original TV shows currently. One of the prominent reason behind this original content is companies removing their content to support own streaming services. Following graph shows year-wise change in addition of Movies and TV shows on the platform. We are showing comparison starting from 2006 and till 2019.

Netflix is losing movies as a result in 2019 number of TV shows surpassed number of movies. For better understanding refer figure 2, here we are showing Netflix’s pattern of uploading content. Red line is representing movies and blue line is representing TV shows.

The number of uploaded content increased steeply after 2016 and increasing very sharply for both movies and TV shows.

United states is highest contributor for movies and TV shows followed by UK. India is lacking behind in case of TV shows. Figure 3 is displaying top 9 countries contributing movies and TV shows respectively.

Mature rated content is highest followed by 14+ and parental guidance. These numbers can easily explain demographics of Netflix users. Paying Netflix users generally belong to 18+ age group, who likes to sit after work for “Netflix and Chill”.
